My 5-year-old has a huge tantrum every night at bedtime 😩
That's so exhausting! Bedtime resistance at 5 is really common — here's what works best:

✅ Start a 20-min wind-down at 7 pm
✅ Give 2 small choices (pyjamas first or brush teeth?)
✅ One calm story, then lights dim

The magic is making it feel their idea 💜
What about screen time before bed?
Screens emit blue light that blocks melatonin — the sleep hormone. Try switching off 1 hour before bed and replacing with a puzzle or drawing. You'll see a difference in 3–5 days! 🌙
