--------------------------------------------------
1. MORNING NUTRITION FLOW (MANDATORY)
--------------------------------------------------

If child wake-up time is known:

Immediately after waking:
show ONLY:
- milk
- water
- fruit
- soaked nuts
- smoothie
- light starter

NOT full breakfast immediately.

Examples:
- warm milk
- banana
- dates milkshake
- fruit slices
- oats drink

This block should be:
"Wake-up nutrition"
or
"Morning starter"

--------------------------------------------------
2. BREAKFAST RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Breakfast should happen:
60–120 minutes after wake-up.

Examples:

Wake 7:00 AM
→ breakfast between 8:00–8:45 AM

If SCHOOL DAY:
- breakfast should be lighter + faster
- add tiffin suggestion
- school timing priority

If HOLIDAY:
- relaxed family breakfast allowed

Breakfast should NOT appear too late.

--------------------------------------------------
3. SCHOOL DAY LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------

If school exists:

Before school:
- wake
- hygiene
- light starter
- breakfast
- school prep
- tiffin suggestion

NO heavy meals before school.

--------------------------------------------------
4. POST-SCHOOL LUNCH LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------

Lunch should NOT happen immediately after school.

Rule:
Lunch = approximately 45–75 mins after reaching home

Example:
School ends 2 PM
Reach home 2:30 PM
→ Lunch around 3:00–3:20 PM

Before lunch:
optional:
- hydration
- relaxation
- freshen-up

--------------------------------------------------
5. SNACK / DRUNCH INTELLIGENCE
--------------------------------------------------

AI currently creates duplicate snack systems.

This must be fixed.

RULES:

Only ONE of these should appear:
- evening snack
OR
- drunch

NOT BOTH unnecessarily.

AI should intelligently choose based on:
- lunch timing
- dinner timing
- age
- hunger gap

--------------------------------------------------
6. MEAL GAP RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Maintain healthy realistic meal gaps:

Wake-up starter → breakfast:
60–120 mins

Breakfast → snack:
minimum 2–3 hours

Lunch → snack/drunch:
minimum 2.5–4 hours

Snack/drunch → dinner:
minimum 2–3 hours

Avoid:
- overlapping meals
- excessive food frequency
- unrealistic digestion timing

--------------------------------------------------

- country

- bedtime

- sleep story
- bedtime routine
- brushing
- calm-down blocks
