Fix and significantly improve the “AI Pronunciation Practice” section inside Amy Speech Coach.

Current Problems:
1. Only a few static words are appearing.
2. Practice content is too limited.
3. Words are not changing dynamically.
4. After child recording, nothing meaningful happens.
5. No AI listening experience.
6. No encouragement or pronunciation correction feedback.
7. Practice session feels incomplete and non-interactive.

Goal:
Make the feature feel like a real intelligent AI speech coach that actively listens, responds, encourages, and guides the child.

Required Improvements:

====================================================
1. DYNAMIC PRACTICE CONTENT SYSTEM
====================================================

Replace the small static word list with a dynamic practice engine.

Requirements:
- Generate large rotating sets of:
  - Letters
  - Phonics sounds
  - Beginner words
  - Advanced words
  - Daily conversation sentences
  - Emotion expressions
  - Tongue exercises
  - Rhyming words

Content should refresh automatically:
- On every session
- On “Next Practice”
- Based on child age
- Based on difficulty level
- Based on previous weak sounds

Examples:
Age 2–3:
- mama
- ball
- cat
- up

Age 4–5:
- butterfly
- elephant
- yellow
- rabbit

Age 6–8:
- “I want to play outside.”
- “The rainbow is beautiful.”

Add:
- Infinite practice generation
- Personalized recommendations
- Randomized sessions
- “Practice More” button

====================================================
2. SHOW MORE CONTENT PER SESSION
====================================================

Currently only 3 words show.

Improve to:
- 8–15 practice items per session
- Scrollable cards/grid
- Auto progression
- Session progress tracker

Add:
- “Easy”
- “Medium”
- “Advanced”

Display:
- estimated session time
- completed count
- streak progress

====================================================
3. REAL AI LISTENING EXPERIENCE
====================================================

After child taps record:
The UI must clearly show that Amy is actively listening and analyzing.

Add:
- Listening animation
- Sound wave animation
- “Amy is listening...”
- “Analyzing pronunciation...”
- AI thinking state
- Voice waveform visualizer

Flow:
1. Child taps mic
2. Recording starts
3. Live waveform appears
4. Stop recording
5. “Amy is analyzing your voice...”
6. AI feedback appears

This should feel alive and interactive.

====================================================
4. REAL FEEDBACK SYSTEM
====================================================

After analysis, Amy must respond intelligently.

Examples:
Positive:
- “Amazing job!”
- “You said butterfly very clearly!”
- “Your B sound is strong!”

Corrective:
- “Try saying the R sound more slowly.”
- “Let’s practice TH again together.”
- “Great try! Put your tongue gently between your teeth for TH.”

Add:
- Highlight difficult sounds
- Detect unclear pronunciation
- Give retry option
- Compare improvement over time

5. ENCOURAGEMENT & CHILD ENGAGEMENT
====================================================

Every interaction should motivate the child.

Add:
- Stars
- XP points
- Celebration animations
- Happy Amy reactions
- Confetti for streaks

Examples:

- “Amy is proud of you!”
- “You improved today!”

Add emotional warmth.
